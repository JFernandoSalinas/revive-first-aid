ABOUT US

Who We Are


Founded in 2018, Revive First Aid offers Emergency and Standard First Aid & CPR courses on-site for local organizations, businesses, child care centres, and schools. Our lead First Aid Instructor Nicole comes with 10 years of first aid teaching experience. She has taught first aid courses in the recreation field, schools, and privately. In addition, she is a trained RECE and lectures Part-Time with the Faculty of Continuing Education at Seneca College. Her hands-on approach will make your organization's first aid training an interactive and valuable experience.
