About Our Courses


At Revive First Aid we are committed to delivering the most up-to-date first aid training. All our courses are affiliated with the Lifesaving Society and are recognized by Ontario's Workplace Safety and Insurance Board, making them a creditable and invaluable training for you and your organization.

Every participant of a Revive First Aid course receives a current Canadian First Aid Manual issued by the Lifesaving Society and a pocket face shield to take home. At the end of the course, each successful participant will receive an instant certification card as proof of certification.

At Revive First Aid we have the flexibility of facilitating first aid courses that work with your organizations availability. We'd love to hear from you to discuss how we can serve you, and to create a course to serve your organizations needs!
