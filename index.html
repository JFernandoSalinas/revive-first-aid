ABOUT US

Welcome to Revive First Aid!


Serving the Greater Toronto Area, including Peel, York, and Simcoe County Regions, Revive First Aid offers Emergency First Aid, Standard First Aid, and CPR courses on-site to local businesses, companies, child care centres, and schools.

All of our courses are affiliated with the Lifesaving Society and recognized by Ontario's Workplace Safety and Insurance Board.
